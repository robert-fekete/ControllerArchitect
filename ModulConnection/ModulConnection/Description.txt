PendulumModulConnection is providing the standard IProcess interface, and and access to a pendulum.
It is covering the differences of the device, so it can be a simulation, or a physical device.

There is three tab of presenter for it:
Log: A logging area for the most important data.
Animation: An animated pendulum which shows the current state of the pendulum.
Graph: A graph which shows the time function of the controlled value.
